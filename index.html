Instructions

Morse Code represents letter and number characters into collections of dits and dahs:

  • The dit is a single unit of length.
  • The dah is equal to 3 dits.
  • The space between the dits and dahs within a character is equal to one dit.
  • The space between characters varies with speed but is generally equal to one dah.

To use "Learn Morse Code":

  • Click on a letter to hear the code.
  • Press the spacebar to tap out dits and dahs.
  • Enter text into the translator to translate a message into Morse Code.
  • Press the Play Translation button to hear your message in Morse Code.
  • Practice tapping along.
